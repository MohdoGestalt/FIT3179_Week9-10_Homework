States and Speed effects on Latency



For most states, there appears to be a negative correlation between internet speed and latency. This indicates that a higher download speed may provide a lower latency in most states, however it is not indicative of the equipment used to test speeds and their affects on the results.

Internet Service Providers



Only Maxis and TM provide DSL internet, while Time only provides Optic Fiber Internet. Time has the best latency but has the lowest speed consistency. The DSL solutions also appear to provide a worse experience with higher latencies.
